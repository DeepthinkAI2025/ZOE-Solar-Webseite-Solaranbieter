## TIER 1: CRITICAL (Sofort - 2-3 Wochen)

### 1. Performance & Core Web Vitals ‚ö°
**Problem:** No image optimization, lazy loading, or font optimization

- Implement CDN caching

**Impact:** +15-25% Lighthouse Score, +20% Conversion  
**Effort:** 2-3 days  
**ROI:** High
